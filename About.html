What is "Astro Studios" and what are they even doing?

What is astro Studios?

Established in 2023 by the dynamic duo of Jxni (Discord: jxnithedev) and Astro (Discord: astrotheguy), Astro Studios is a hub for crafting tailor-made Discord bots. Our passion for coding and creativity has led us to not only create bespoke bots for our clients but also to manage two unique Discord bots of our own.Our team recently expanded with the addition of Tucky (Discord: tuckydev), a versatile developer who started with us from the beginning, bringing a fresh perspective and skill set to our collaborative efforts.At Astro Studios, we believe in pushing the boundaries of what Discord bots can achieve. Explore our custom solutions or test out our beta bot to experience the latest in bot technology. Join us on this exciting journey where every line of code is a step toward a more dynamic and engaging Discord experience.
